How to Style a Quiff

To style a quiff, start with clean, damp hair. Apply a volumizing mousse and blow-dry while lifting the hair at the roots. Use a round brush for volume. Once dry, apply a small amount of pomade or wax. Shape the quiff upwards and back, then finish with a strong-hold hairspray.
